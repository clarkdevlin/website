Corporate Profile

ECICS Limited (ECICS) was founded in 1975 specialising in Trade Credit, Bonds and Guarantees. It is a wholly owned subsidiary of IFS Capital Limited (IFS) listed on Singapore Stock Exchange (SGX) and is licensed general insurer regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS).

Over the years, ECICS has expanded its product offerings to include coverage for Directors & Officers, Professional Indemnity, Property, Foreign Worker Medical, Public Liability, Fire, Marine and Accident & Health for business clients in varying industries.

ECICS is also known in the industry for its Auto insurance for both private and commercial use. Apart from Auto insurance, ECICS caters to retail consumers from all walks of life with comprehensive coverage for Foreign Domestic Worker, Personal Accident, Building & Home Contents and Hospitalisation.

A.M. Best Rating

Rated "B++" (Good) for financial strength and an "bbb+" for issuer credit rating by A.M. Best.
